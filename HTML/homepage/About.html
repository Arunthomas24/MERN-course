About Our Business

History

Founded: 2010

We began as a small family-run poultry farm supplying fresh eggs and table birds to local markets. Over the years we've invested in improved housing, biosecurity, and feed formulations to increase productivity while maintaining animal welfare standards. Today we supply a network of retailers, restaurants, and community markets across the region.

Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, nutritious, and affordable poultry products while promoting sustainable farming practices. We aim to support local food systems by producing high-quality eggs and meat, reducing waste, and continuously improving animal health through responsible husbandry and vaccination programs.

Our Values

  • Animal welfare: humane treatment and comfortable housing for all birds.
  • Food safety: strict hygiene and traceability from farm to table.
  • Sustainability: efficient resource use and waste minimization.
  • Community: supporting local jobs and transparent relationships with customers.
