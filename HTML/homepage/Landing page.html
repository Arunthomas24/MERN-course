        Poultry farming is the practice of raising domesticated birds like chickens, 
        ducks, and turkeys for their meat, eggs, or feathers. 
        It is a significant agricultural activity that provides food and income, 
        with the two primary sectors being broiler farming for meat and layer farming for eggs. 
        Key aspects include providing proper housing, nutrition, and healthcare, 
        and the commercial scale can range from small to large operations
